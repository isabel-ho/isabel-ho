Dr. Hologram

Leveraging Holoportation Science for Improved Health Care Outcomes

Harnessing the power of shared immersive holographic experiences to train future health care providers, staff, caregivers and community in humanistic care.

The World’s first and only single passenger HOLOPORTATION MACHINE.

Capture the humanity of an individuals lived experiences, understand their disability, go further stepping into human health to teach our future health care providers humanistic care.

Develop a series of labs in order to train future clinicians the symptom profile, social emotional implications of disease and disability, understand the treatment path and triaging scenarios.

Besides live holoportation, Dr. Hologram can also film, produce, and edit any content or subject matter and play it back on a loop or through motion activation as a hologram movie.

Live action story telling that addresses the biopsychosocial aspects of lived experiences of individuals with disabilities.

Provides real time experiences for physicians and other health care providers to evaluate and treat these individuals.

Can be implemented in large or small group settings, with interdisciplinary teams or for direct patient care.
